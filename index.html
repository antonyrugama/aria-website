The Problem

Training alone shouldn't mean training blind.

Most solo sprinters are guessing. No second set of eyes, no structured feedback loop, no one managing the full picture. The result? Wasted sessions, hidden weaknesses, and preventable injuries.

No eyes on your mechanics

You can't watch yourself sprint. Without real-time form feedback, bad habits compound silently — costing you tenths you'll never get back.

No plan that adapts to you

Static PDFs and cookie-cutter programs don't know your recovery status, competition schedule, or whether yesterday's session was too much.

No one managing the full picture

Training, nutrition, recovery, rehab, mental readiness — solo athletes juggle all of it alone. Drop one ball and performance suffers. Or worse, you get injured.

Recovery & Rehabilitation

Stay healthy. Come back stronger.

Aria tracks recovery metrics, flags injury risks before they become problems, and provides rehabilitation protocols. Integrated with Apple Health, Samsung Health, Strava, and Garmin.

  • Recovery readiness score from HRV, RHR, and Sleep
  • Injury status tracking: Healthy, Minor, Recovering, Injured
  • Dedicated rehabilitation section with guided protocols
  • Health Trends with directional arrows over time
  • Cycle tracking for female athletes
Cycle Tracking — Built for Female Athletes

Her body. Her phases. Her edge.

Aria is one of the only sprint training apps that integrates menstrual cycle tracking directly into training and nutrition recommendations. Because female athletes deserve programming that adapts to their biology — not ignores it.

  • Phase-aware training adjustments (Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulation, Luteal)
  • AI recommendations adapt intensity, volume, and recovery to each phase
  • Nutrition guidance shifts with your cycle — macros, hydration, and micronutrients
  • Daily logging: energy, pain, mood, bloating, flow, and cramps
  • Cycle ring visualization with phase indicators and day tracking
